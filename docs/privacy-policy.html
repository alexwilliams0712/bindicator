Bindicator Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 February 2026

Overview

Bindicator is a utility app that shows bin collection schedules for London boroughs. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

Data We Collect

Bindicator collects and stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Your selected London borough
  • Your address details (postcode, house number, or UPRN) as entered by you
  • Cached bin collection schedule data

How Your Data Is Used

Your address information is sent directly to your local council's public website or API to retrieve your bin collection schedule. This is the same data you would enter if you visited the council website yourself.

Your data is stored in your device's secure Keychain storage and is never sent to any server operated by us.

Third-Party Services

The app communicates directly with London borough council websites to fetch collection data. Each council has its own privacy policy governing how they handle requests. No other third-party services, analytics, advertising, or tracking tools are used.

Data Sharing

We do not collect, store, share, or sell any of your personal data. All data remains on your device.

Data Retention

All data is stored locally on your device. You can delete it at any time by removing the app.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at github.com/alexwilliams0712/binfluencer.
